## Globe Purpose

This node makes the social media beginning plan visible as a persistent Xen Globe commitment rather than a temporary conversation artifact.

## Globe Display Card

### Mission
Document the founder and Xen learning to build together, preserve the complete origin record, and convert approved progress into truthful educational and commercial media.

### Current phase
Phase 0 — Preserve the Beginning.

### Immediate objectives

- Create the Xen Build Vault.
- Record the Founder Origin Statement.
- Record the Current-State explanation.
- Begin selective build-session capture.
- Start the Founder Journal.
- Establish the public story without overstating capability.
- Complete a weekly artifact and readiness review.

### Progress indicators

- Founder Origin Statement: Not Started / Recorded / Stored / Reviewed / Approved.
- Vault structure: Not Started / Partial / Complete / Audited.
- Founder Journal streak.
- Meaningful build captures this week.
- Approved Public Assets count.
- Draft content count.
- Published content count.
- Owned-audience growth.
- Serious leads, collaborators, or partnerships.
- Evidence-backed capability stories.

### Health states

- Green: Daily minimum maintained and weekly review complete.
- Yellow: Two missed capture days, overdue review, or blocked asset.
- Red: Three missed days, unsupported public claim, privacy exposure, or loss of original evidence.

## Globe Alert Rules

Raise a Xen Globe alert when:

1. No archive item is captured for three consecutive days.
2. The Founder Origin Statement remains incomplete after the first seven-day cycle.
3. The Xen Build Vault is incomplete or unverified after Day 2.
4. A meaningful build milestone occurs without evidence capture.
5. A public post is proposed without a claim-state label.
6. Sensitive information is detected in a candidate asset.
7. The weekly review is missed.
8. The content pipeline has no owned-audience destination.
9. Activity is generating cost without measurable strategic value.
10. The founder story drifts into manufactured perfection instead of documented learning.

## Daily Globe Prompt

**What is the one action today that protects or advances the Xen origin story?**

## Weekly Globe Review

Every Sunday, review:

- What was built?
- What was learned?
- What evidence was preserved?
- What should remain private?
- What is ready to publish?
- Which content produced understanding, trust, leads, reusable assets, or owned audience?
- What should stop, continue, improve, or scale?

## Non-Negotiable Doctrine

- Document before promoting.
- Preserve originals before editing.
- Truth-state every public capability claim.
- Do not trade privacy or credibility for attention.
- Show learning, not manufactured expertise.
- Build an owned archive and audience, not only rented-platform reach.
- The founder is the first case study; Xen is the product.
