# XMI Daily Bread Integration Contract

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Active integration contract  
**Canonical authority:** `decureton-xencreator/xen-operating-system/architecture/xmi/XMI-001-XEN-MUSIC-INTELLIGENCE-ARCHITECTURE-v1.0.md`

Daily Bread may consume Xen Music Intelligence as an optional daily experience layer.

Permitted experiences include:

- Song of the Day;
- Sound of the City;
- Emerging Artist Spotlight;
- Local Scene Pulse;
- mood-appropriate morning, commute, focus, evening, or weekend listening;
- one-tap continuation into an authorized provider;
- optional cultural context, never compulsory instruction.

The default experience must remain low-friction. Music recommendations must not clutter the report, interrupt primary priorities, or force explanations. The user may simply play music.

Location use must be permissioned or manually selected. Sponsored content must be labeled. Daily Bread must preserve private-by-default listening history and inherit the XMI principle: **Music first. Intelligence second. Education only when invited.**

This repository must not duplicate or redefine XMI architecture. It implements only the Daily Bread presentation and interaction contract.
