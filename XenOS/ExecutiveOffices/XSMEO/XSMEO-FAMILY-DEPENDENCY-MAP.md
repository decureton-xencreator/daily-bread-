# XSMEO Family Dependency Map

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Canonical / Active  
**Updated:** 2026-07-18

## Family Members

- XSMEO-001 — Xen Social Media Executive Office
- XSMEO-002 — Xen Living Rollout Experience
- XVE-001 — Xen Value Engine

## Direct Dependencies

### XSMEO depends on

- XOS for runtime, authorization, lifecycle, observability, and governance
- XPF for foundational principles and architecture inheritance
- XKR for runtime registration and orchestration
- XVE for monetization, spending optimization, value measurement, and Leave Everything Better review
- XSoT and canonical repositories for truthful source material and provenance
- Xen Lexicon for canonical names, acronyms, aliases, and public terminology
- Identity, Trust, Privacy, Security, Legal, and Governance controls
- XOC for dashboards, campaign health, alerts, and executive review
- XRDL Experiment Registry for campaign and creative experiments
- Project Phoenix and XEE for learning, diagnosis, evolution, and reuse
- Xen Vault for raw historical footage, artifacts, and publication controls
- Xen Globe and website for canonical destination, archive, conversion, and world-state visualization

### XVE depends on

- XOS, XPF, and XKR for authority and execution controls
- XOC for metrics and operational state
- XSoT for verified evidence and financial source data
- Money Tree for capital, portfolio, and financial planning
- XSOE for opportunity discovery
- XSMEO for audience, media, demand generation, and social commerce
- Xen Business systems for sales, pricing, operations, customer success, and profitability
- Project Phoenix, XEE, and XRDL for experimentation and continuous improvement
- Legal, tax, accounting, privacy, security, and trust controls

- Every Executive Office
- Xen Academy

- Enterprise deployments

## Event Relationships

- Approved architecture milestone -> XSMEO content-opportunity assessment
- Verified product capability -> rollout and demonstration assessment
- Campaign proposal -> XVE value hypothesis and spending review
- Campaign launch -> XRDL experiment registration and XOC monitoring
- Audience response -> Community Intelligence and product-discovery input
- Campaign completion -> Executive AI Auditor and Leave Everything Better review
- Validated learning -> XSoT, Lexicon, playbook, and Project Phoenix update
- Material architecture change -> System-Wide Synchronization

## Publication-State Dependency

No public campaign may advance beyond the actual system state. Public language must remain synchronized with one of these states:

Vision -> Concept -> Canonical Specification -> Prototype -> Build in Progress -> Demonstration -> Pilot -> Validated Capability -> Production Capability -> Historical Artifact

## Family Governance Rule

XSMEO optimizes attention, trust, community, narrative, and demand. XVE optimizes measurable value. Neither may override truthfulness, user welfare, safety, privacy, law, governance, or long-term mission integrity.

## Immediate Implementation State

The architecture, rollout model, founder proof-of-concept doctrine, platform-family roles, XVE integration, monetization review, ad-spend optimization, content-state controls, archival requirements, measurement framework, and cross-system dependencies are approved and active at the specification level.

Operational execution still requires platform accounts, brand assets, production workflows, analytics connections, budget authority, publishing approvals, and validated product demonstrations.
