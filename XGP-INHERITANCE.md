# XGP Inheritance — Daily Bread

**Status:** Active derivative synchronization  
**Canonical authority:** `decureton-xencreator/xen-operating-system/Foundation/AM/AM-006.md`

Daily Bread is a first-class Xen Globe Platform capability.

It shall evolve into a bespoke, spatial daily intelligence layer that illuminates where meaningful changes occurred, why they matter to the individual user, and what authorized actions may follow.

Required behaviors include user-specific prioritization; geographic and contextual relationships; links to people, projects, businesses, family, travel, learning, and purpose; clear distinction among fact, inference, urgency, and confidence; progressive disclosure; and privacy, consent, and user control.

The integration target is not a generic news map. It is a living contextual briefing surface within the user's intelligent operating environment.
