## Operating Rules

1. Preserve originals before editing.
2. Date every artifact.
3. Record uncertainty honestly.
4. Separate private material from approved public assets.
5. Do not publish unsupported capability claims.
6. Protect sensitive information, identities, credentials, and private conversations.
7. Capture major breakthroughs, failures, corrections, and unanswered questions.
8. Review the Vault during the Sunday Xen Weekly Review.
